WSGIKit

WSGIKit is an implementation of Webware, decomposed into WSGI middleware.

It currently lives in a subversion repository at http://svn.w4py.org/WSGIKit/trunk -- but it will probably be moved someplace else in the future, maybe get a real page and everything.

Discussion about WSGIKit should take place on webware-devel@lists.sourceforge.net
