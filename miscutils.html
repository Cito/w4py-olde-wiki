MiscUtils

MiscUtils is a collection of useful classes and functions for general use. Unlike most Webware components, it does not constitute an object-oriented framework. Pick and choose what you like.

The online docs are at: http://webware.sourceforge.net/Webware/MiscUtils/Docs/

DataTable and Configurable are my favorite classes in MiscUtils.
