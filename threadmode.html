Thread-mode

A style of Wiki writing that facilitates an ongoing discussion between interested parties. Each party owns their entries, which are signed and dated. See http://www.usemod.com/cgi-bin/mb.pl?ThreadMode for a fuller explanation. It is complemented by DocumentMode, which is like a synopsis of synthesis of a discussion.
